## 1) X Thread (10 tweets max)

```
1/ Most people think building wealth requires hours of work.
Wrong.

15 minutes a day beats 3 hours once a year.

2/ Step 1: Define the outcome

List every account. Every obligation. Every recurring fee.

You can't improve what you don't measure.

One app. One account structure. One habit.

5/ Step 4: Automate it
Remove willpower from the equation.

If it requires a decision every time, it will fail.

Only change course on data, not headlines.

Emotional trading kills wealth. Systems build it.

7/ Common mistake: chasing the "best" product instead of building a repeatable process.

The best system you'll actually use beats the "perfect" system you won't.

8/ Another trap: checking balances daily.
You'll overcorrect.

Weekly or monthly reviews only.

9/ Stress-test your plan.
Job loss? Market drop? Rate spike?

Make sure you have buffer cash or insurance for downside scenarios.

10/ Build the system. Automate it. Let compounding do the heavy lifting.

Start today. 15 minutes.
```
