## 1) X Thread (10 tweets max)

```
🧵 Building a Wealth Routine in 15 Minutes a Day

Most people fail at wealth-building not because of markets or rates.
They fail because they're reacting emotionally every single day.

Here's the framework that changes that 👇

---

Step 1: DEFINE THE OUTCOME
What's your real target?
- A specific dollar amount?
- Debt reduction?
- Risk management?

Get clear. Everything else flows from this.

---

Step 2: QUANTIFY THE BASELINE
You can't improve what you don't measure.
List every account, obligation, and cash flow stream.
Know where you stand today.

---

Step 3: PICK ONE SYSTEM
Not 5 apps.
Not 10 accounts.
One tool. One structure. One habit.

Simplicity beats sophistication every time.

---

Step 4: AUTOMATE EXECUTION
Remove willpower from the equation.
Set transfers. Set contributions. Let it run.

Decision fatigue kills more wealth-building than market crashes.

---

Step 5: REVIEW QUARTERLY
Only change course based on DATA, not headlines.
15 minutes every 90 days beats 15 minutes every single day.

The difference? You're directing the ship, not reacting to waves.

---

⚠️ Common mistakes to avoid:
- Chasing the "perfect" product
- Adding risk before stabilizing cash flow
- Checking balances obsessively
- Ignoring taxes, fees, and insurance gaps

Start with one. Build it. Automate it. Let compounding work.

That's it. 🚀
```
