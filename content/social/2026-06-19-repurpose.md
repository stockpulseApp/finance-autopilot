## 1) X Thread (10 tweets max)

```
🧵 Building a Wealth Routine in 15 Minutes a Day

1/ The highest-leverage move isn't picking the "best" investment. It's building a repeatable system that stops you from reacting emotionally. People who do this see clearer decisions within 30 days.

2/ Here's the 5-step framework:
• Define the outcome (what $ are you targeting?)
• Quantify baseline (where are you today?)
• Pick ONE system (one app, one account, one habit)
• Automate execution (remove willpower)
• Review quarterly (data, not headlines)

3/ Week 1: Audit everything
List every account & obligation. Cut one redundant fee. Schedule 20 min weekly money review. That's it.

4/ Week 2: Build the core
Open/optimize your main tool. Set automatic transfers. Write a one-page checklist of your rules. Done.

5/ Week 3: Stress-test
Run a downside scenario (job loss, market drop). Confirm you have buffer cash. Adjust positions if needed.

6/ Week 4: Optimize & scale
Compare results to target. Add ONE improvement only if the first is stable. Share the system with an accountability partner.

7/ Biggest mistakes to avoid:
❌ Chasing perfect products
❌ Taking risk before stabilizing cash flow
❌ Checking balances daily
❌ Ignoring taxes & fees

8/ The truth: Consistency beats intensity. Build it, automate it, let compounding work. Your future self will thank you.
```
