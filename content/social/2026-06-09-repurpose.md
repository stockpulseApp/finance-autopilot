8/ Other mistakes to avoid:
- Increasing risk before cash flow is stable
- Checking balances daily
- Ignoring taxes, fees, insurance gaps

9/ Real talk: Wealth rewards consistency over intensity.
Build it. Automate it. Let compounding work while you sleep.

10/ Start this week. Pick ONE action. Then automation does the rest.
Consistency compounds. Always has.
