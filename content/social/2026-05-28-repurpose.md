## 1) X Thread (10 tweets max)

```
🧵 Building a Wealth Routine in 15 Minutes a Day

Most people fail at money because they're reacting, not systematizing.

Here's the 4-week framework that changes that:

1/ WEEK 1: AUDIT & ALIGN
List every account. Cut one redundant fee. Schedule one 20-min money review weekly.

That's it. You're already ahead of 80% of people.

2/ WEEK 2: IMPLEMENT CORE MECHANICS
Open your primary tool. Automate transfers. Document your rules on one page.

Automation removes willpower from the equation. This is where real change happens.

3/ WEEK 3: STRESS-TEST
Model a downside scenario (job loss, rate spike, market drop).
Confirm you have buffer cash or insurance.
Adjust position sizes if needed.

Don't hope. Prepare.

4/ WEEK 4: OPTIMIZE & SCALE
Compare results to your target metric.
Only add improvement #2 once improvement #1 is stable.
Find an accountability peer.

Consistency > intensity. Let compounding do the heavy lifting.

5/ The biggest mistakes:
❌ Chasing perfect products instead of building repeatable systems
❌ Taking risk before stabilizing cash flow
❌ Checking balances daily and overcorrecting
❌ Ignoring taxes, fees, insurance gaps

Build systems, not habits.

Ready to start? Your 15-min routine compounds into serious wealth. The question isn't *if* you have time—it's whether you're ready to be systematic about money.
```
