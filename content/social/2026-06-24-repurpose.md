1/ Building wealth doesn't require hours of grinding. It requires 15 minutes of *structured* daily routine. Here's the framework we use to help people stop reacting emotionally to money 🧵

2/ The outcome: Clearer financial decisions within 30 days. Not because markets cooperate, but because you're following a system—not your emotions.

3/ Step 1: **Define the outcome**
What's the actual target? A number? Risk reduction? Freedom from a specific debt?
Be specific. Vague goals = vague results.

4/ Step 2: **Quantify your baseline**
Map every account, obligation, and cash flow stream. You can't optimize what you don't measure.

5/ Step 3: **Pick ONE system**
One app. One account structure. One habit. More tools = more friction. Simplicity is the cheat code.

6/ Step 4: **Automate everything**
Remove willpower from the equation. Automatic transfers, contributions, bill pays. Set it and forget it.

7/ Step 5: **Review quarterly**
Change course on data, not headlines. Market noise is not a reason to abandon your system.

8/ Common trap: Chasing the "perfect" product instead of building a repeatable process. The best tool is the one you'll actually use.

9/ Your 15-minute routine should include:
- Weekly money review (20 min)
- Quarterly stress tests
- System optimization only when stable

10/ Consistency beats intensity. Build the system. Automate it. Let compounding handle the rest. Ready to start? Begin with week 1: audit + align.
