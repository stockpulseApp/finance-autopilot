## 1) X Thread (10 tweets)

```
🧵 Building a wealth routine doesn't require hours. 15 minutes a day is enough.

Here's the framework we use:

1. DEFINE THE OUTCOME
What's your target? A dollar amount? Risk reduction? Be specific.

2. QUANTIFY THE BASELINE
List every account, debt, obligation. You can't improve what you don't measure.

3. PICK ONE SYSTEM
One app. One account structure. One habit. Avoid the paradox of choice.

4. AUTOMATE EXECUTION
Remove willpower from the equation. Set it and forget it.

5. REVIEW QUARTERLY
Change course on data, not headlines. Emotional reactions = wealth killer.

Week 1: Audit everything. Cut one redundant fee.
Week 2: Automate transfers. Document your rules.
Week 3: Stress-test scenarios. Confirm your safety net.
Week 4: Compare results. Add ONE improvement only if stable.

The biggest mistake? Chasing the "perfect" product instead of building a repeatable process.

Consistency beats intensity. Build the system, automate it, let compounding do the work.

Start with 15 minutes this week. Your future self will thank you.
```
