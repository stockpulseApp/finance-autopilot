## Preview: Utilization without carrying balances

Reporting utilization is usually based on **statement balance**, not whether you pay in full.

| Tactic | How it works |
|--------|----------------|
| **Pay before statement close** | Low reported balance even if you use cards heavily |
| **Multiple payments per month** | Smooths spikes from large purchases |
| **AZEO (All Zero Except One)** | Advanced — one card reports small balance, others $0 |

**Example:** $10,000 total limits, $3,000 statement balance = 30% utilization. Paying down to $500 before close can report 5% — often a quick score bump if that was your bottleneck.

## Preview: Disputes that are worth filing

Dispute **inaccurate** items only — fabricated disputes waste time and can backfire.

- Wrong late payment dates  
- Accounts that are not yours  
- Paid collections still showing open  
- Duplicate tradelines  

The paid guide includes **FCRA-compliant letter templates** and a tracking log.
