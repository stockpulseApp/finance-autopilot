# Side Income Launch Map

**Preview — full map includes pricing calculators and outreach scripts.**

The goal is not “start a business.” The goal is **$1,000/month in net income** from something you can sustain alongside a job — then decide if you scale.

---

## Preview: Validate in 7 days (no website required)

| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | Write 3 problems you can solve for people like you |
| 2 | Pick one — interview 5 people (15 min each) |
| 3 | Offer a **paid pilot** ($50–$200), not free work |
| 4–5 | Deliver pilot, collect testimonial |
| 6 | Price full offer from pilot feedback |
| 7 | Send 20 targeted outreach messages |

**Kill criteria:** Nobody will pay → pivot offer, not branding.

---

## Preview: Pricing services

Use **value anchor**, not hourly guilt:

```
Price = (client outcome $) × 10–20% capture
```

Example: You save a freelancer 5 hours/month at $80/hr → $400 value/mo → charge $79–149/mo retainer.

**Paid sections:** proposal template, Upwork/LinkedIn DM scripts, scope creep boundaries, simple LLC vs sole prop overview (CPA for your state), and tax set-aside formula.
