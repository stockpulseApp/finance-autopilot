## Preview: The four numbers that matter

| Metric | What it tells you | Rule of thumb (market-dependent) |
|--------|-------------------|----------------------------------|
| **Cap rate** | NOI ÷ price | Compare to local averages, not national tweets |
| **Cash-on-cash** | Annual pre-tax cash flow ÷ cash invested | Must beat your next-best use of down payment |
| **DSCR** | NOI ÷ debt service | Lenders often want ≥ 1.20–1.25 |
| **Breakeven occupancy** | Costs ÷ gross rent | Stress-test vacancy above your market norm |

**Worked example (simplified):**  
$220,000 purchase, 20% down, $1,650/mo rent, $400/mo operating expenses (ex-debt), 7% rate 30-year loan → you calculate NOI, then cash flow after debt, then cash-on-cash. The full guide shows three scenarios: base, vacancy shock, rate +1%.
